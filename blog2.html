FOR PERFECT ATMOSPHERE




THESE ITEMS WILL CREATE THE PERFECT ATMOSPHERE IN YOUR HOME

As a place where you should escape to after a hectic day of work, your home needs to evoke an atmosphere that can help you relax and recharge from the day. So in today’s busy world, it is more important than ever to create a calming atmosphere that allows you to relax and recharge. With so many different styles and decor available, it can sometimes be difficult to know where to begin when decorating your home. No matter what your design style or lifestyle you live, these items will create the perfect atmosphere in your home.



COMFORT IS KEY

As we have realised the importance of comfort in our homes, incorporating quality furniture into your designs can help you create a more relaxing atmosphere. That is why choosing a linen sofa is the first step into creating a calming atmosphere. Linen feels cool and smooth, making it a popular upholstery choice for any climate. Additionally, linen is also appreciated for its durability. If you’re looking for an upholstery fabric that lasts for years, it doesn’t get much much better than linen. Our linen sofas will offer your space style as well as a laid back atmosphere and will be the place you want to sit back and relax in after a long day. All our linen sofas are hand crafted with perfection and built with quality in mind. The sofas offer a wooden frame, providing resilience. The linen upholstery is removable, meaning it can be washed or replaced whenever you want, perfect for a family.

UNIQUE PIECE

When you welcome guests into your home, they should instantly know that this is your space. Having a unique item or piece of furniture in your living space adds style whilst showcasing your personality. Try adding a unique piece of furniture such as a stylish, comfortable lounge chair. Lounge chairs are no longer seen as just seating, but can create comfort and a luxurious touch to your space. We offer a large selection of different lounge chairs that you can choose from according to your taste and style. From boho lounge chairs to an elegant Scandinavian style, we have unique lounge chairs for your home.


WARM LIGHTING

Light is more than just a way to illuminate a room. Adding the right kind of lighting to your home can make a significant difference in your health, mood and the atmosphere around you. If you want to create a specific mood in your home, lighting is always the way to do it. You can make a room cozy and warm or fresh and minimalistic. Every type of lighting that you integrate into your home should add a dash of your personality.
Table lamps are one way of creating a laid back atmosphere in your home. They can be functional and stylish. Many of our table lamps have a simple and stylish design and are a perfect way to add a classic accent to your home decor. Table lamps are perfect to place in the living room with your sofa, adding soft light to your reading nook or creating coziness to your bedroom.

ADD A RUG

Adding a rug changes the focal point of any room and certainly adds a visual beauty that every home deserves. It instantly creates a space more cozy, comfortable and can pull together various colours used in the decor. They are versatile and all rugs can be incorporated into a variety of different design styles.

As summer has arrived, a jute rug would be the perfect accent piece in your home. Jute rugs are created with natural fibers, providing a stylish, raw texture to your home. In addition to this, jute is a fibre that is environmentally friendly which also provides your home with a calming atmosphere.
